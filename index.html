How this works

The problem, plainly

Your NFT is on Ethereum permanently. But the actual image and metadata files live on IPFS, kept alive by Foundation's servers. When Foundation goes dark, those servers stop. The art file can disappear. Your token still exists but points to nothing. This tool pins your files somewhere you control before that happens.

[01]

Enter your wallet address

Paste your public address or connect your wallet. We never ask you to sign anything. No transaction. Nothing spent.

[02]

We find every Foundation NFT you've ever minted

We scan Foundation's shared contract and your personal collection contracts. This finds everything: held pieces, listed pieces, and sold pieces.

[03]

Pin your files free

Click "Pin all free" and we handle everything server-side via Pinata. Your files get pinned to IPFS via Pinata, independent of Foundation's servers. Free, permanent, no cost to you.

[04]

Delist listed pieces if needed

If you want to move listed pieces to a new platform, delist them on Foundation first using the Delist button on each card. Foundation is building a bulk tool. Until then, open listings one at a time. Each delist requires a small gas fee.

[05]

Move forward

Art safe. List on Zora or Manifold today. Or wait for canvas, a new platform being built right now with zero fees, forever.

Foundation is currently still pinning your files until April 15, 2027. This tool creates your own independent backup so you are not reliant on them.
