How this works

The problem, plainly

Your NFT is recorded on Ethereum permanently. But the actual image and metadata files live on IPFS, kept alive by Foundation's servers. When Foundation goes dark, those servers stop. The art file disappears. Your token still exists but points to nothing. This tool pins your files somewhere you control before that happens.

[01]

Enter your wallet address

Paste your public address or connect your wallet. We never ask you to sign anything. No transaction. Nothing spent.

[02]

We find your Foundation NFTs

We query Foundation's public Ethereum contract and find every piece you created. Each one has a content identifier pointing to your files on IPFS.

[03]

We pin your files — automatically

Click "Pin all free" and we handle everything server-side via Filebase. Your files are copied to a redundant pinning node that you own, independent of Foundation. Costs nothing.

[04]

Download your metadata

Grab a JSON backup of each piece. Holds your title, description, and all file identifiers. Keep it somewhere safe.

[05]

Move forward

Art safe. Tokens still yours on Ethereum. List on Zora or Manifold today, or wait for canvas — a new platform being built with zero fees, forever.

Foundation is currently still pinning your files until April 15, 2027. This tool creates your own independent backup copy so you are not reliant on them. Better to act now.
